  This is the list of my favorite Dinos.

Tyrannosaurus Rex

Tyrannosaurus rex, commonly known as T. rex, was a massive, bipedal carnivore that lived during the Late Cretaceous period. This iconic dinosaur, the namesake of the tyrannosaurid family, was renowned for its powerful jaws, strong bite force, and fearsome appearance. T. rex was one of the largest known land carnivores, reaching lengths of up to 40 feet and weighing up to 8.8 tons. Its size, combined with its powerful bite and keen sense of smell, made it a formidable predator.

Triceratops

Triceratops, meaning "three-horned face," were large, herbivorous ceratopsian dinosaurs that lived during the Late Cretaceous period, approximately 68 to 66 million years ago. They are easily recognized by their three horns and a large, bony frill extending from the back of their skull. These dinosaurs were one of the last known non- avian dinosaurs to exist before the Cretaceous-Paleogene extinction event.
