How Web Storage Helps in Games

localStorage and sessionStorage are used in games to save player data directly in the browser.

- localStorage stores game progress, high scores, or settings like sound and difficulty level. The data stays saved even after you close the game or the browser.

- sessionStorage is used to keep temporary data like your current level or active session. When the game tab is closed, this data is cleared.

These features make browser-based games more fun and smooth by remembering your progress and choices without needing an account.
